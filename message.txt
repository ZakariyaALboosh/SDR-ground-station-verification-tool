SDR Ground Station Verification Tool
=====================================
This is a small test payload transmitted over a simulated LEO pass.
It exercises the full pre-deployment pipeline: TLE-based orbit
propagation, dynamic Doppler, a link budget, BPSK with convolutional
coding, a CRC-protected framing format, and an SDR-style receiver.
If this exact text is recovered at the other end, the design works.
